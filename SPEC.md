## Purpose

This root specification tracks SpeckKit governance for the project.

Detailed feature-level specifications are maintained under [specs/](specs/), including the active pipeline in [specs/001-form-extraction-pipeline/spec.md](specs/001-form-extraction-pipeline/spec.md).

## Scope

In scope:
- Governance and project-level standards for form extraction and processing.
- Traceability to detailed feature specifications under [specs/](specs/).

Out of scope:
- Repeating all feature-level details already maintained in individual spec artifacts.

## Requirements

- The project must maintain current feature specifications under [specs/](specs/).
- Governance documents listed in [SYSTEM_MANIFEST.json.md](SYSTEM_MANIFEST.json.md) must stay in sync.
- Any implementation changes must preserve requirements defined in active feature specs.
